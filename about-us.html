About Us

Our Mission

At Bohr Systems, our mission is to revolutionize modern defense with cost-effective autonomous drone swarms. Due to our drive for innovation and dedication to national security, we push the limits of autonomy in advanced robotics. We are committed to reducing human risk and optimizing battlefield performance. Our solutions empower American forces with precision, reliability, and strategic superiority. The future of defense lies in automated tactical warfare. Bohr Systems is at the forefront of this evolution, creating powerful solutions that keep American soldiers out of harms way.

The Founders

Rohil Gharat

Rohil Gharat, co-founder and CEO of Bohr System, is a successful serieal entrepreneur. After taking a sabbatical from college to pursue entrepreneurship, he founderd MathChimp and Hats4Pali, two highly profitable ventures. With over five years of experience in autonomous robotics, he specializes in the intersection of robotics and technology-driven business. After extensive research in unmanned systems, he shifted his focus to advancing autonomous drone technology. Beyond his technical expertise, he demonstrates strong leadership and communication skills, establishing himself as a key figure in defense innovation.

Samarth Kumbla

Samarth Kumbla, co-founder and CTO of Bohr Systems, is a Computer Science major at Columbia University with a strong background in software development. Through his past experience in software engineering at startups, his ability to navigate complex technical challenges in early-stage startups has positioned him as a leader in the entrepreneurial landscape. Beyond his academic and professional pursuits, Samarth is also a world-class professional fencer, representing the United States in the international stage. As a decorated athlete, he led the U.S. team to Gold and Bronze medals at the 2023 and 2024 World Championships, showcasing his dive and resilience.
