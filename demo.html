Solution Overview

The Problem

Drones can't fly in GPS-jammed environments. Current solutions often fail in low visibility, rendering over 160,000 drones useless and costing over $9.73B annually.

Our Solution

L-NAV is a clip-on, LIDAR-based navigation module that allows drones to operate as if GPS was still available. It attaches to the bottom of a drone, enabling autonomous flight in low-light, GPS-jammed environments.
