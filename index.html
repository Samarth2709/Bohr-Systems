Bohr Systems

Building Autonomous Drone Swarms in GPS-Denied Enviroments

Problem

The majority of the DOD’s “autonomous” vehicle solutions are contingent on constant soldier communications and GPS. This over-reliance on outdated systems exposes a serious vulnerability when it comes to GPS-contested/signal-jammed areas.

Learn more

Our Solution

We utilize two drones: a mother drone and strike drone. Our asychronous autonomous system uses a combination of sensors, LiDar, SLAM, and IR rays to locate a target, create a simulated trajectory, and send a barrage of tactical strike drones with pinpoint accuracy to neutralize the threat.

Learn more

Demand

In adjacent reports, the DOD have repeatedly stated high interest in GPS Denied Solutions, Autonoumous Swarm Drones, and Human-Machine Interoperability in order to combat modern warfare.

Learn more

Product Features

Mother Drone

  • Manually operated
  • Designed to locate target and curate flight path for strike drone
  • LiDar, IR Laser, HD Camera

Strike Drone

  • Autounomously Operated
  • Recieves and follows cureated flightpath to strike the marked target
  • Explosive Device, IR Sensor, RTB Functionality
